Reverse Engineer to Catia V5-6

In this project, I utilized CATIA V5-6 to reverse engineer an existing aircraft called Purcara. The process involved carefully analyzing the physical components and structure of the plane, then translating these details into a 3D model within CATIA. This project required high precision and attention to detail to accurately replicate the design. By capturing the geometry and features of the Purcara, the reverse-engineered model served as a basis for further analysis, design improvements, and potential modifications.
