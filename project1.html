Landing Gear Assembly with CATIA V5-6

This project focused on designing and assembling an aircraft's landing gear system using CATIA V5-6. It involved precise part modeling, followed by the integration of components into a functional assembly. By utilizing CATIA's simulation tools, particularly laws, I analyzed the mechanical behavior and dynamics of the landing gear under various conditions. The simulations provided valuable insights into the system's performance, ensuring its reliability and effectiveness in real-world applications.
