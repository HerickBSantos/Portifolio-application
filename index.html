About Me

Hello! I am Herick Santos, an Aerospace Engineering Technologist student with a passion for designing and analyzing aircraft systems. Welcome to my portfolio, where I showcase my projects and skills. I am an enthusiastic Aerospace Engineer with a passion for the aviation and aerospace environment. My objective is to contribute to the development and advancement of aircraft technology through innovation and continuous growth.

I excel in using advanced design and manufacturing software such as CATIA V5, SOLIDWORKS, and MASTERCAM. I have solid experience with avionics components, composites manufacturing, and materials assembly. My skills include geometric dimensioning and tolerancing, CNC machining, and basic programming in Python, CSS, and HTML. Additionally, I am proficient in Microsoft Office, which allows me to create detailed reports, documents, presentations, dashboards, and tables.

My achievements include a Gold Medal at the Brazilian National Astronomy Olympiad and a Silver Medal at the Brazilian National Science Olympiad. I am committed to leveraging my technical expertise and innovative problem-solving abilities to drive excellence in aerospace engineering.
