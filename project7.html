Assembly and drawing of solidworks various parts.

In this project, I developed an assembly drawing in SolidWorks, incorporating GD&T to precisely define the geometric and functional requirements of the components. The drawing includes a detailed assembly of parts, complete with dimensions, tolerances, and annotations to ensure proper fit and function. GD&T symbols were used to specify the allowable deviations in the geometry of each component, enhancing the accuracy of the manufacturing and assembly processes. This approach ensures that all parts meet the necessary standards for quality and performance, facilitating effective communication and production.
