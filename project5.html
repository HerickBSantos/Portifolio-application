Design of a single Mastercan Piece, using 3d geometrical features.

In this project, I developed a simple mastercan part in Mastercam for machining. The design involved a unique shape with specific features and details, intended to be used as a reference fixture. The focus was on setting up the part for accurate and efficient machining operations, including turning and drilling, to ensure precision and consistency in the final product.
