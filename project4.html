Turbine Blade on Mastercan and 5-axis CNC

In this project, I programmed the machining process for a turbine blade using Mastercam. The project involved creating a detailed toolpath for a CNC mill with 5-axis capabilities, ensuring precision in the machining of complex geometries. After the program was completed and optimized, I oversaw the manufacturing process, where the turbine blade was successfully machined to exact specifications on a 5-axis CNC mill. This project demonstrated my proficiency in advanced machining techniques and the use of CNC technology for high-precision manufacturing.
