Draft of a part in Mastercam

In this project, I followed a draft for creating a part in Mastercam. The draft provided detailed guidelines on the part's geometry and machining requirements, which I used to set up the machining operations. This included defining the key features and dimensions for accurate turning and drilling processes. By adhering to the draft, I ensured that the part was produced according to the specified design and machining standards.
