Fixture for Drilling, Global 7500

In this project, I designed a custom fixture for the vertical stabilizer of a Bombardier Global 7500. The fixture was specifically engineered to assist in the precise drilling of holes, ensuring accuracy and consistency during the manufacturing process. The design considered the unique contours and structural integrity of the vertical stabilizer, allowing for efficient and secure attachment while minimizing potential errors during drilling operations. This fixture played a critical role in enhancing productivity and maintaining quality in the assembly process.
