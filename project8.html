Solidworks Drone Full Project

In this project, I designed and assembled a drone in SolidWorks, creating all the individual parts and producing detailed assembly drawings. Each component, including the frame, propellers, motors, battery mount, and control systems, was modeled to precise specifications. After completing the parts, I used SolidWorks to assemble them into a fully functional drone, ensuring proper fit and alignment. The assembly drawings included detailed views, dimensions, and GD&T annotations to communicate the required tolerances and geometric relationships, ensuring accurate manufacturing and assembly of the drone components.
